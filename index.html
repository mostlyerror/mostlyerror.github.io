Karis Tang‑Quan & Nicholas Ong

10.26.2019

  • Our Story

      
    According to Nik

    I had no luck connecting with someone despite going on dates over the years. The online dating scene turned into a routine of swiping left and passing everything on the apps. Occasionally, there would be someone that would pique my interest on Coffee Meets Bagel, but I had to pay to connect. Karis was one of those people because she looked cute, played sports, and traveled. To my delight, she responded, and we connected over texts.

  •   
    According to Karis

    What stood out to me from our first date was that Nik made me laugh and shared authentically about his life. When I got home, I immediately started debriefing with my parents about how I thought the date went. While we were chatting, Nik sent me a clever text that I read to my parents, and it made both my dad and me laugh.

    After our first dinner, Nik and I continued to text and go on dates, which led to occasional phone calls, and eventually to a conversation about our relationship. In October 2017, we decided to make our relationship official and went on our (second) first date!

  • We're Engaged!

      
    05.04.2019

    Throughout our dating, we both shared a common interest in escape room games with friends and family. This gave Nik the idea to have an escape room at his house with hints and clues about their experiences and relationship. Under the guise of an already scheduled date night, Nik asked Karis to come over, and when she was on her way, Nik texted her about her mission to save the universe from the dreadful Thanos by finding the stolen Infinity Stone hidden in Nikâ€™s house.

    Karis went through each puzzle leading her to the missing Infinity Stone (an actual rock) and to the keys of her escape car. As the garage door opened, Nik greeted her, congratulating her on finding the Infinity Stone only to inform her that she had the wrong stone.

    Karis looked at Nik with confusion and surprise as he knelt on one knee to present her the real stone she had been searching for. With an enthusiastic yes from Karis, Nik had finally reached his endgame and the universe was saved!
